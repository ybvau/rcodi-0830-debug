NEW PUBLICATION BY DR. BRUNSWICKER IN RTM

Last updated on Feb 22, 2021 1 min read news

     In a study that surveyed large firms in Europe and the United States, Dr. Brunswicker and Dr. Henry Chesbrough (UC Berkeley) examine the extent to which large firms are practicing open innovation.  As the world continues to move towards a more openly innovative environment, businesses, too, continue to adopt more open innovation practices. This study will be published in the March-April issue of the Research-Technology Management (RTM) Journal. To see this article, please go to Research‐Technology Management (RTM) online at www.iriweb.org/rtm.
