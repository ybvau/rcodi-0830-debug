KICKOFF OF THE PURDUE RED IRONHACKS

Last updated on Feb 22, 2021 1 min read news

Tomorrow at 5:00 PM in DLRC, RCODI will kick off it’s first training session for the IronHacks. This is your chance to learn about open data, how to do cool visualizations, and hack to create cool and novel applications for society! There is still time to sign up for this event and all you need to bring is: 1) A Laptop 2) Your creativity and inspiration 3) Energy and good mood! Address: Discovery Learning Resource Center, RM 143A/B, 207 South Martin Jischke Drive, West Lafayette, IN 47907 If you have questions please e-mail us at opendigital@purdue.edu and visit our Facebookpage.
